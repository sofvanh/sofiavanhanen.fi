Sofia Vanhanen

Building technology for a future full of flourishing

I'm a computer scientist and software engineer working primarily in AI R&D, but my stack and interests are vast; I've built collective reasoning platforms, social media apps, multiplayer games; web, mobile, desktop, backend. I view software and AI as tools for uplifting, empowering, liberating, and connecting society.

I'm co-founder and CTO at Mosaic Labs. We build experimental LLM interfaces for AI-mediated collective reasoning and deliberation, conducting research into how AI assistance can help humans coordinate.

I work with many groups on projects that range from AI interfaces to digital democracy and futures studies. Please reach out if you'd like to work with me.
